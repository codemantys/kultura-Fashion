📏 Use a soft measuring tape and measure close to your body without pulling too tight. Have someone help you for accurate measurements.
Chest / Bust Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape parallel to the floor. Waist Measure around your natural waistline — typically the narrowest part of your torso, about 2.5cm above your navel. Hip Stand with feet together. Measure around the fullest part of your hips and bottom, about 20cm below your waist. Shoulder Width Measure from the end of one shoulder to the end of the other, across the back. Inseam Measure from your crotch to the bottom of your ankle bone along the inner leg. Height (Children) Stand your child straight against a wall, feet flat on the floor. Measure from the top of the head to the heel.
